Social Media App Information

Hopper is a social media app developed with JSP and an MySQL database. It allows users to connect and share content with friends and followers. The app offers features such as creating profiles, posting updates, sharing photos and videos, and interacting through comments and likes. The JSP framework enables dynamic web pages, providing a seamless user experience. The MySQL database efficiently stores user information, posts, and interactions, ensuring data integrity and fast retrieval. Hopper aims to enhance social connectivity and foster a vibrant community of users.
